Discord Airdrop

This airdrop utilizes a method named Zero Knowledge Proof that allows you to claim participation in the governance system based on your Discord ID without revealing any personal information. You can learn more about the ZKP airdrop method here.

How does it work?

1. Generate your Key, Secret, & Public ID.

Use your cursor to randomly generate a new Key and Secret pair which you'll download for later use, then get a Public ID derived from your Key and Secret.

2. Share your new Public ID on Discord.

Share your new Public ID on Discord to associate it with your Discord ID.

3. Check your eligibility.

A new link will be shared on April 11, 2022 where you'll check if your Discord ID was eligible by entering your Key and Secret.

Encryption

Drag the slider below to generate your key and secret

Move your cursor in any direction while dragging the slider to generate a new key and secret. Once the progress bar is completely full, download the JSON file containing your new key and secret before continuing to the next step.
